This folder holds the summary and excerpt text files and the poem files.

The subfolders books and poems seperate the two classes of files.

Book files are named:
    book-title-summary.txt
    book-title-excerpt.txt
    
Poems are named poem-name.txt

Words in titles are seperated with hyphens and all lowercase is used as in:

american-woman-suspended-summary.txt
american-woman-suspended-excerpt.txt

a-woman-at-the-steinway.txt
